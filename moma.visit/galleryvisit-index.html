MOMA VISIT ON A SUNDAY

You stop because everyone else stops.
A famous painting is somewhere ahead.

Phones rise into the air.
Someone blocks the label while reading it aloud.

You nod slowly.
You are not sure why.

Oil on canvas, 1931
Permanent Collection

The room is full.
The silence is not.
